About

The Artiverse

The Artiverse is a hub for emerging artists. Not a gallery that charges you to exhibit. Not a platform that aggregates thousands of profiles without distinction. A project founded by an artist, for artists — born out of two years of paid open calls, inaccessible exhibitions, and a system that too often puts wallets before talent.

Our premise is simple: if your work is interesting, you deserve to be seen. Full stop. Not after paying a submission fee. Not after proving you already have 10,000 followers. Now.

"99% of open calls share one thing in common: you pay to be seen. We exist to change that."

We are a digital hub with a strong editorial identity — every artist we present is told with care, not just published. Over time we will also become physical, with exhibitions and events. But the direction is always the same: the artist at the centre.

What we offer

What you get
with us

We don't promise the moon. We promise what we can deliver — and we deliver it. Here's what goes into your profile on The Artiverse.

Curated profile

A page dedicated to your work — not a template identical for everyone. A text written by us that tells who you are, what you do and why you're worth following.

Instagram visibility

Dedicated posts on the @the_artiverse feed — with carefully written captions that add context and point of view. Not a simple repost: a presentation.

Authentic storytelling

We ask you to share your process, not just the result. We also present work in progress, ongoing series, research. Living art, not only finished art.

A real community

You join a network of selected artists — not a database, a community. Mutual support, cross-visibility, and a place where you're not alone.

And: no exclusivity. You can keep submitting to other platforms, participating in other exhibitions, doing whatever you want. The Artiverse doesn't ask for loyalty — it offers space.

The process

How it
works

No bureaucracy, no endless forms. The process is simple and transparent — you always know where you stand.

1

You apply

Fill in the application form (link below). We ask: who you are, a link to your work, 3–5 pieces that represent you, and a couple of lines on what you're looking for. Not a CV, not a formal letter. Be yourself.

2

We review

We look at your work carefully. No infinite waiting lists or editorial committees. We respond within 2 weeks, always — even if the answer is no. If it's no, we tell you why.

3

We get to know you

If you're selected, we connect — DM or a short call — to better understand your work and what you want to tell. We don't put you in a database: we build a story together.

4

You go live

We publish your profile and your first post on the channel. We share the link with you — you can spread it however you like. You are the protagonist, we are the stage.
